Year In Review - Customer Report

2024 Year In Review Report WelcomeHome Software

I led the creation of the 2024 Year In Review report, the biggest annual piece of collateral from the customer insights function. This year, I focused on driving key technical improvements—like process automation, ensuring data fidelity, and leading feedback efforts with our vendor. By streamlining production and juggling multiple timelines, I was able to boost both scalability and quality. The report draws from real-time data across thousands of communities in primary, secondary, and tertiary markets, covering all 50 states, with insights from millions of prospects and customers using WelcomeHome’s CRM.

We expanded our analysis, integrated more feedback, and revamped marketing content to better tell the story behind the data. I also led webinars to share key takeaways and best practices, helping senior living leaders make informed decisions. This content played a crucial role in the prospect journey, aligning with Sales to convert prospects into customers.

Collaboration was essential this year. Working closely with Marketing, Sales, and CX, I helped solve technical challenges and strengthen feedback loops, delivering actionable insights to help the senior living industry thrive in 2025.
